Henry Lau
Born October 11, 1989
Occupation: Singer, Songwriter, Composer, Actor

Biography

Henry is a Canadian who debuted as a member of Super Junior-M in 2008. He is mostly active in South Korea and China. He knows how to play the violin, piano, guitar, drums, and loop station. He made his solo debut in 2013. He began playing piano at the age of 5 and the violin at the age of 6. He is also fluent in English, Mandarin, Korean, French, and Cantonese. He also acted in a few korean dramas and have went on to have major roles in movies and dramas in Korea, China, and Hong Kong.
